Designing a new dam

At the farm-level, the performance of a new dam tailored for a specific farm system and climatic conditions can be simulated with the results integrated into cost-benefit analysis and farm planning tools.

Regional-scale vulnerability assessments

For water planners, government officials, consultants, and researchers the WET-tool can be used for large-scale simulations of dam performance.

For example, the failure rate of all dams in an LGA could be simulated to provide a large-scale view of water vulnerability and geographically target interventions to boost dam performance.
